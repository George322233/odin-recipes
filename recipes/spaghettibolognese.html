Sphaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Back to home

How to make the worlds tastiest Spaghetti bolognese!

Ignredients:

  • Beef
  • Sphaghetti
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozerella cheese

Method:

  1. Get the beef and start to cook in pan
  2. Do this for 5 min until nearly all light brown in colour
  3. Add tomatoes
  4. Layer in baking tray with laters of lasagna between, ensure it is even
  5. Add mozerella cheese ontop, a nice and even layer
  6. Cook in oven for 25mins on 200c fan oven
  7. Once slightly browned ontop, remove lasagna and serve!
